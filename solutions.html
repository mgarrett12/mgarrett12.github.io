Affordable Housing

We envision a Portland where all citizens can find affordable homes in healthy neighborhoods with strong schools, good parks and recreation, healthy natural areas, safe streets and quality food stores; and have equitable access to housing and to the opportunities that safe, stable housing can deliver, free from discrimination. Our goal is to increase housing options for low-income people by addressing the largest gaps in affordable housing.
